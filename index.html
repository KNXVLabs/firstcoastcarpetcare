Why Encapsulation?

Not Your Typical Steam Clean

Most carpet cleaning companies use hot water extraction (steam). It works, but it soaks your carpet — and a wet carpet for 24+ hours is a breeding ground for mildew, odors, and re-soiling.

Encapsulation uses a low-moisture polymeric formula that surrounds dirt particles, crystallizes them, and makes them easy to vacuum away. The result is a deeper clean that stays cleaner longer — without the downsides.
